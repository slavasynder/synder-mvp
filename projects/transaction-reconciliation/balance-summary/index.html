What to look at
  • Balance status pill in the header — three states next to the existing "Not reconciled" pill.
  • Balance Summary block above the tabs — 5-line compare (Beginning + Change = Computed end vs. Reported end → Delta), always visible across tab switches.
  • Delta line as a launchpad — investigate links switch to Discrepancy / Not matched tabs without losing block context.
  • Collapse-on-match — when delta = 0, the block collapses to a single confirmation strip; click to expand.
  • State toggle in the top-right (off-by / matches / no beginning balance) lets you walk reviewers through all three states.
