How to get Account Transactions from Xero
▼
1
Open Xero Reporting reporting.xero.com → Account Transactions
2
Select accounts: Clearing, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Realized Currency Gains
3
Pick the date range that matches the reconciliation date range
4
Set Grouping to Group by Account
5
Select columns: Contact, Date, Description, Net, Reference, Running Balance, Source. If multi-currency is enabled, also add: Currency, Net (Source), Net ((currency)).
6
Export as Excel
Tip: save the report settings as a Custom view in Xero to avoid this next time.
