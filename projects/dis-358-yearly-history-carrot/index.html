DIS-358 — Free 1yr historical data for yearly plans

PLG yearly conversion is averaging 31.9% over the past 24 months. This mock explores three promotional surfaces for offering 1 year of historical data free on Basic & Essential yearly subscriptions — without changing the core plan structure.

Interactive mock
Plan page with variant switcher

Toggle between billing cycles and switch between four promotional variants in real time.
What's in the mock
  • Current — faithful recreation of today's "Choose your plan" page. Visual baseline.
  • Variant A — Toggle badge: a small "Includes 1yr free history" tag under the yearly toggle. Cheapest surface; catches anyone who looks at the toggle.
  • Variant B — Card callout: a green block inside the Basic & Essential cards announces the offer. Strong, plan-specific, only shows on yearly.
  • Variant C — Monthly modal: clicking Subscribe on Basic/Essential while on monthly interrupts with a switch-to-yearly modal. Targets only the wavering ones.
  • All combined — stakeholder demo of every surface stacked. Production would A/B one or two.
Mechanics in all variants
  • On yearly: Basic & Essential history line swaps from "Paid unlimited history" → "1 year of historical data — free".
  • On monthly: history line reverts to "Paid unlimited history". No promo shown.
  • Pro & Premium are unchanged (Pro is SLG, not the PLG conversion target).
  • Bottom banner copy adapts: yearly mode reframes it as "additional history beyond 1 year" rather than the headline offer.
Open question: attach rate of historical add-on among current Basic/Essential yearly subs. Without it, this is a directional bet — the variant choice doesn't change, but the success target does.
