Why this is its own iteration
  • DIS-259 ships the Balance Summary block; the auditor report adds a new deliverable surface, source-file attachments, PDF generation, and "Send to auditor" delivery — meaningfully larger build than the balance check itself.
  • Slava's conversion-lever idea: locking the report behind a completed reconciliation (no Discrepancy, no Not-matched; Ignored and Matched are fine) gives users a visible payoff for finishing — strong candidate for a follow-up Opportunity.
  • Picking the right activation event (e.g. TxnReconciliationFinalReportDownloaded) is a natural anchor for that follow-up Opportunity's success metric.
