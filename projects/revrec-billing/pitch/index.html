Story 1 of 10

Billable Subscription Counting Logic

Core backend — what makes a subscription "billable"

What's Billable
✅ Has at least one non-zero invoice (not trial-only)
✅ Has current or future recognition periods
❌ Trial subscriptions excluded (zero invoices only)
❌ Subscriptions with only past recognition periods excluded
Two Counting Aspects
🔴 Live Counter

Real-time billable count for current billing period. Powers the usage meter (Story 5) and limit enforcement (Story 6).

🔵 Historical Attribution

Subscription counted only in periods where it was genuinely billable. No retroactive smearing. Powers billing history (Story 9).

📅 Important: Billing periods are anchored to plan start date (e.g., Mar 15 – Apr 14), NOT calendar months. Period boundary handling is the most complex piece of this story.

Story 6 of 10

Subscription Limit Enforcement

What happens when an org exceeds their plan limit

When Limit is Hit
✅ Existing subs within limit → stay "Ready"
🔴 New incoming subs above limit → "Paused"
⚡ Active billable count must always fit within plan limit
🔄 Auto-upgrade ON: next tier purchased automatically, no interruption
⏸️ Auto-upgrade OFF: subs paused until manual upgrade
Paused Subscription Behavior
❌ RevRec journal entries STOP generating
❌ No recognition schedule generated or visible
❌ Not included in RevRec reports
✅ Normal transactions still pass to Synder Sync
✅ Synder Sync continues working normally
✅ Data still flows — only JEs blocked
🔄 On upgrade / un-pause: RevRec JEs generated retroactively for the paused period. No data loss — recognition catches up automatically.

Story 8 of 10

Downgrade / Cancel Flow

Safely reducing plan tier with pausing safeguards

Downgrade Rules
→ Downgrade to any lower tier, minimum = free included amount (Pro: 1,000 · Premium: 5,000)
→ If downgrade causes over-limit: excess subs marked Paused automatically
→ System enforces: active billable count ≤ new plan limit at all times
→ Paused subs: Synder Sync continues normally (Story 6 rules apply)
🚫 Full cancel NOT available self-serve — requires new org. UI shows explanation.
UX Warning Before Confirmation
